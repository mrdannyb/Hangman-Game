Welcome to the deadliest known game: Hangman

In this game, the lonely stick person prays that you can guess the word in the alloted number of tries. The rules are pretty simple:

  • Type any letter to attempt a guess.
  • A wrong guess counts against you, and a correct one will fill in the word.
  • If you finish the word, congratulations! This stick person lives and we let them go.
  • If you don't... well let's just say that our stick people fear their cruel overlords *que laughter*
